Baba Ramdev Smarak Post Graduate College, Bartar, Nonhara, Ghazipur (U.P.) is a premier institution of higher education committed to fostering academic excellence, research, and holistic development of students.

Affiliated to Veer Bahadur Singh Purvanchal University, Jaunpur, NCTE approved for B.Ed. and D.El.Ed. (BTC) programmes and affiliated to SCERT. The college holds UGC recognition under Section 2(f) & 12(B).
