Right to Information Act, 2005

In compliance with the Right to Information Act, 2005, the college has designated Public Information Officers (PIO) and Appellate Authority to respond to RTI applications.

How to File an RTI Application

  • Write an application clearly specifying the information sought
  • Address it to the Public Information Officer (PIO), BRSPGC
  • Attach a court fee stamp of ₹10 or DD/Postal Order in favor of the college
  • BPL applicants are exempted from the fee
  • Response will be provided within 30 days of receipt of application
