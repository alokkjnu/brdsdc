How to File an RTI Application

  1. Write an application in Hindi or English addressed to the Public Information Officer (PIO)
  2. Attach an application fee of ₹10 (by cash/DD/postal order) – BPL applicants are exempt
  3. Submit in person at the college office or send by post/email
  4. Response will be provided within 30 days of receipt
  5. For life & liberty matters, response will be provided within 48 hours
