Health Care Services

The college is committed to the health and well-being of all students and staff.

Campus Medical Facilities

  • First aid room equipped with basic medical supplies
  • A trained first aider available on campus during college hours
  • Periodic health check-up camps organized for students
  • Eye check-up camp (annual)
  • Blood donation camps with health screening

Tie-up with Nearby Hospitals

  • District Hospital, Ghazipur – 5 km from college
  • Primary Health Centre, Nonhara – 1 km from college
  • In case of emergency, college vehicle is available for transport

Mental Health & Wellness

  • Student counseling cell for academic and personal issues
  • Regular yoga and meditation sessions
  • Awareness programs on mental health, stress management
  • Anti-ragging squad for immediate assistance

Health & Hygiene Awareness

  • Swachh Bharat Abhiyan activities
  • Sanitation and hygiene awareness drives
  • Safe drinking water facilities throughout campus
  • Regular fumigation and pest control
