Sports & Co-Curricular Activities

The college encourages students to participate in sports and co-curricular activities for holistic development.

Sports Facilities

  • Cricket Ground with pitch preparation facilities
  • Athletics Track for running and field events
  • Volleyball Court (outdoor)
  • Basketball Court
  • Kabaddi and Kho-Kho grounds
  • Indoor games: Table Tennis, Chess, Carrom

Annual Sports Events

  • Inter-Class Sports Tournament (September)
  • Annual Sports Day (February)
  • Inter-College Sports Tournament participation
  • University level games representation

Co-Curricular Activities

  • Debate and Elocution Competitions
  • Quiz competitions (General Knowledge & Subject-specific)
  • Essay Writing Competitions
  • Cultural programmes – Drama, Dance, Music
  • Annual College Magazine publication

Achievements

  • Several students have represented the college at university-level sports meets
  • College has produced state-level athletes in Cricket and Athletics
