How to Apply

  • Register on the scholarship portal (scholarship.up.gov.in or scholarships.gov.in)
  • Fill in the scholarship form online with correct details
  • Upload required documents (marksheet, income certificate, caste certificate, bank details)
  • Submit through college and get forwarded to the competent authority
  • Last date for application – usually September–October each year

Required Documents

  • Caste Certificate (SC/ST/OBC)
  • Income Certificate (issued by Tehsildar / SDM)
  • Previous year mark sheet
  • Bank account (linked with Aadhaar)
  • Domicile / UP resident certificate
  • Enrollment / Fee receipt from college

For any scholarship-related assistance, contact the college scholarship cell or enquiry counter.
