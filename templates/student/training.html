Training Programs

The college offers various training and skill development programs to enhance employability and career readiness of students.

Career Guidance & Placement Cell

  • Career counseling sessions for final-year students
  • Resume writing and interview preparation workshops
  • Campus recruitment drives in collaboration with local industries
  • Guidance for competitive examinations (UPSC, SSC, UP PCS, etc.)

Teacher Training (B.Ed. / D.El.Ed.)

  • Teaching Practice at affiliated schools for 2–4 weeks
  • Micro-teaching sessions and demonstration lessons
  • School internship programme
  • Educational psychology and classroom management training

Skill Development Programs

  • Computer basics and MS Office training
  • Communication skills and spoken English workshops
  • Entrepreneurship Development Program (EDP)
  • Digital literacy and social media awareness

Special Programs

  • Yoga and meditation workshops (monthly)
  • First Aid and disaster management training
  • Legal literacy workshops for students
