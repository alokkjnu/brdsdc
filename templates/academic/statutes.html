Statutes & Ordinances

The college follows the statutes and ordinances prescribed by Veer Bahadur Singh Purvanchal University, Jaunpur, UGC, NCTE, and SCERT as applicable.

Governing Documents

  • University Act and Statutes of VBSPU, Jaunpur
  • UGC Regulations on Minimum Standards and Procedures for Award of M.Phil./Ph.D. Degrees
  • NCTE Regulations for B.Ed. and D.El.Ed. programmes
  • SCERT Uttar Pradesh – D.El.Ed. regulations
  • UGC Act 1956 – Section 2(f) and 12(B)
  • Right to Education Act, 2009

College Rules & Regulations

  • All students must maintain 75% attendance in each subject to appear in examinations
  • Ragging in any form is strictly prohibited and punishable
  • Students must follow the college dress code on campus
  • Mobile phones are not permitted in classrooms and examination halls
  • Students are responsible for maintaining discipline and decorum on campus
  • Any grievance should be submitted to the Grievance Redressal Committee
