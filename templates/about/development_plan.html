College Development Plan

The College Development Plan outlines the strategic roadmap for institutional growth, academic enhancement, and infrastructure development over the next five years.

Short-Term Goals (2024–2026)

  • Upgrade computer laboratory with latest hardware and software
  • Expand library holdings to 20,000+ books and digital resources
  • Install smart classrooms with projectors and interactive boards
  • Strengthen placement cell and industry linkages
  • Enhance sports infrastructure including outdoor courts

Long-Term Goals (2026–2029)

  • Apply for NAAC accreditation for institutional quality certification
  • Introduce new vocational and skill-based programmes
  • Establish a dedicated Research & Innovation Centre
  • Build a new hostel block for outstation students
  • Develop an e-learning platform for digital education delivery

Infrastructure Development

  • Construction of new academic block with modern classrooms
  • Renovation and expansion of existing laboratory facilities
  • Development of green campus with solar energy systems
  • Accessible ramps and facilities for differently-abled students
