About AISHE

AISHE (All India Survey on Higher Education) is an initiative by the Ministry of Education to collect data on higher educational institutions across India. This data helps in policy planning and resource allocation for education.

Our college diligently updates its AISHE data every year to ensure accurate representation in national statistics.
