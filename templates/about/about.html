About the College

Baba Ramdev Smarak Post Graduate College (बाबा रामदेव स्मारक स्नातकोत्तर महाविद्यालय) is located at Bartar, Nonahara,Ghazipur, Uttar Pradesh. The college is dedicated to providing quality higher education to the students of the region.

The college is affiliated to Veer Bahadur Singh Purvanchal University, Jaunpur (U.P.) and holds College Code 0513. It has been granted recognition under Section 2(f) & 12(B) of the UGC Act 1956, making it eligible for central grants.

The B.Ed. and D.El.Ed. (BTC) programmes are approved by the National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE) and are affiliated with the State Council of Educational Research and Training (SCERT), Uttar Pradesh.

Key Facts

  • College Code: 0513
  • Affiliated University: Veer Bahadur Singh Purvanchal University, Jaunpur (U.P.)
  • UGC Recognition: Section 2(f) & 12(B) of the UGC Act 1956
  • NCTE Approved (B.Ed. & D.El.Ed./BTC)
  • SCERT Affiliated (D.El.Ed./BTC)
  • Location: Bartar, Nonahara,Ghazipur (U.P.) 233303

Our Commitment

The college is committed to nurturing young minds and empowering them with knowledge, values, and skills necessary to thrive in a competitive world. With experienced faculty, modern infrastructure, and a student-centric approach, we strive for academic excellence at every level.
