Fee Refund Policy

The fee refund policy of the college is as per the UGC guidelines and university norms. Students seeking withdrawal of admission may claim refund as follows:

Date of CancellationRefund %Deduction
15 days before the start of classes100%Only processing fee (₹ 500) deducted
Before the last date of admission but after commencement of classes80%20% of tuition fee deducted
Within 30 days after last date of admission50%50% of tuition fee deducted
After 30 days from last date of admission0%No refund

Refund Process

  • Submit a written application for withdrawal/cancellation to the Principal
  • Attach all original fee receipts with the application
  • Refund will be processed within 15 working days
  • Refund will be made via NEFT/RTGS to the student's bank account

Important Note

No refund shall be applicable for students who have been expelled or whose admission has been cancelled on disciplinary grounds.
