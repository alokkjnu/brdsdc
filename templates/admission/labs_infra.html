Laboratories & Infrastructure

The college is equipped with modern laboratories and infrastructure to support quality education.

Science Laboratories

  • Physics Laboratory – Equipped with modern instruments for practical experiments
  • Chemistry Laboratory – Well-equipped with glassware, chemicals, and safety equipment
  • Biology / Botany / Zoology Lab – Microscopes, specimens, and research tools

Computer Laboratory

  • 50+ computers with latest configuration
  • High-speed broadband internet (100 Mbps)
  • Licensed software for academic use
  • Dedicated for B.Ed., D.El.Ed., and other programmes

Classroom Infrastructure

  • 15 spacious classrooms with proper ventilation and lighting
  • 3 smart classrooms with projectors and interactive boards
  • Seminar hall with seating capacity of 200
  • Examination hall with 500 seats

Sports & Recreation

  • Outdoor cricket ground and athletics track
  • Volleyball, basketball, and kabaddi courts
  • Indoor games room with table tennis, chess, etc.

Other Facilities

  • Administrative block with well-furnished offices
  • Girls' common room with safe and secure facilities
  • Reprography and printing centre
  • Drinking water purifiers throughout the campus
