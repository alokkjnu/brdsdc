Library Membership & Services

New students are enrolled as library members as part of the admission process. The library provides comprehensive academic support.

Library Membership Process

  • Library membership is automatic upon college enrollment
  • Library card will be issued after fee payment and enrollment
  • Lost library cards can be reissued on payment of ₹50

Available Services

  • Book lending (up to 2 books per student)
  • Reference section with encyclopedias and dictionaries
  • Reading room for quiet study (8 AM – 5 PM)
  • Internet facility for academic research (limited sessions)
  • Newspaper and magazine reading section
  • Photocopying service available at nominal cost
