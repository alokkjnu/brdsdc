The college follows the reservation policy as prescribed by the Government of Uttar Pradesh and the affiliated university.

Important Notes

  • All certificates must be issued by competent authority
  • OBC certificate must be of Non-Creamy Layer (valid for current year)
  • EWS certificate must be issued for the current financial year
  • Unfilled reserved seats may be converted to general as per university norms
  • Horizontal reservations are applicable across all categories
